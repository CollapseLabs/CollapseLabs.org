Online Social Learning

We want to recognize and support all of the social learning activities happening across the Internet. Specifically we’re interested in the kind of learning that is

  • for adults (lifelong learning),
  • informal (not credential-earning),
  • social (not books and podcasts),
  • peer-to-peer (not teacher-led), and
  • online (Internet-based).

Examples include learning communities, study circles, book clubs, and mentoring initiatives.

We’ve started to catalog these below. We hope that you find this useful, and if you know of something that we’ve missed, please fill out the form or otherwise contact us.
